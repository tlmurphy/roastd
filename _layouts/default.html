Project Goals.

Roastd is an app where coffee lovers can share, discover, and rate drinks from local coffee shops and coffee roasters. Users are able to check in and rate different drinks and review beans they purchased from coffee roasters across the nation.

Roastd gains insight from coffee drinkers with similar palettes and uses collaborative filtering to recommend certain drinks or beans to users based on their ratings or review history. The app also allows coffee shop owners and coffee roasters to directly engage with their customers and the local community.

1

Provide a platform where users can rate and keep track of drinks they have tried.

2

Introduce a unique recommendation system that creates a user-tailored "playlist" of drinks to try.

3

Build a community where coffee shop owners and coffee lovers can connect and share.

Project Features

  • Favorite beans, shops, and drinks.
  • View favorited items.
  • Search for beans, shops, and drinks.
  • Generates recommendations based on user's coffee "palette".
  • View drink recommendations.
  • Discover new drinks associated with shops.
  • Discover the shops behind your favorite drinks and beans.
  • Get directions to local coffee shops via Google Maps.
  • Become shop "managers" for shops you own.
